What we're about

Three Rules,
That's It.

01 / SPEED

Ride Fast

Performance EUCs, tuned scooters, the occasional esk8 — if it's got a motor and a top speed worth bragging about, we're riding it.

02 / DISTANCE

Ride Far

Weekday commutes turn into 40km detours. Weekend rides turn into cross-city loops. Range anxiety is just a challenge.

03 / FREQUENCY

Ride Often

Night rides, weekend meetups, spontaneous Tuesday sessions — if there's a reason to ride, we're already out the door.

How to collaborate

If It's Fun,
We're In.

Most of us have day jobs — this is what we do for fun. So the bar for working with PEV Squad isn't a media kit, it's one question: will it be fun? Get that right and we're already halfway there.
